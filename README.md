## üöÄ Project Overview

**ServiceNow Oversight Engine** is a platform governance and monitoring tool that gives organizations **full visibility into their ServiceNow instance**. It identifies risky customizations, technical debt, and CMDB inconsistencies, providing **health, risk, and compliance metrics** to executives, platform owners, and architects.

- **CIOs and CTOs** ‚Äì Require a high-level view of platform health, risk, and compliance to make strategic decisions.  
- **ServiceNow Platform Owners** ‚Äì Responsible for managing technical debt, upgrade readiness, and governance of the platform.  
- **ServiceNow Administrators / Architects** ‚Äì Need actionable insights to maintain system integrity, optimize performance, and prevent risky customizations.  

> In short: Anyone responsible for **platform health, governance, and decision-making**.

---

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Core Problems Addressed

1. Lack of visibility into **risky or inefficient customizations**.  
2. **Incomplete or inconsistent CMDBs**, leading to poor decision-making and operational risk.  
3. Platform **performance issues go unnoticed** until they impact users.  
4. Upgrade planning is **manual and risky**, with no automated assessment of technical debt.  
5. Enterprises spend **thousands on audits and consulting** due to the absence of automated governance tools.

---

## üí° Solution

The ServiceNow Oversight Engine **automates platform governance and health monitoring** by:

- Scanning the ServiceNow instance for risky customizations and technical debt.  
- Validating **CMDB consistency and trust** metrics.  
- Providing a **Health Score**, **Risk Score**, and **Compliance Score**.  
- Generating **dashboards for executives and admins** with actionable insights.  
- Suggesting **remediation steps and upgrade readiness guidance**.
